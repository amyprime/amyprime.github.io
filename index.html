Here are some pages I have produced for Fair Districts PA. Please note that these pages are mostly intended to be embedded inside a larger page, not viewed standalone.

  • The redistricting slider shows the difference between former and proposed district maps.
  • The legislator lookup informs people about their current representative's voting history.
  • The "know before you vote" page informs people about their local candidates.
  • The resolutions map allows people to explore whether their municipality or county has passed a resolution in favor of fair districts.
