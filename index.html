Here are some pages I have produced for Fair Districts PA.

Please note that these pages contain minimal formatting because they are intended to be embedded inside a larger page.

  • The redistricting slider shows the difference between former and proposed district maps.
  • The "contact your legislator" page provides current legislators' contact information and voting history.
  • The "know before you vote" page informs people about the candidates in their local elections.
  • The resolutions map allows people to explore whether their municipality or county has passed a resolution in favor of fair districts.
